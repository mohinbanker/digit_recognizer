True or False: A buyer's earnings in a round will be lower if they pay a higher price.

Correct!

The correct answer is: True.

Buyers will always receive tokens at the end of a round by giving the object they purchased to the experimenter. However, in order to buy the object, they will have to pay the price set by the seller. Paying a higher price means that buyers will be left with fewer tokens at the end of the round.

True or False: A seller's earnings in a round will be higher if they set a higher price.

Correct!

The correct answer is: It depends.

Since there are {{ subsession.sellers }} seller{{ subsession.sellers|pluralize }} in each group, there is no guarantee that a seller will sell an object — a higher price may lead buyers to buy from {{ subsession.sellers|pluralize:"the ,an"}}other seller.

However, if a seller does sell an object, then a higher price will earn the seller more tokens.
